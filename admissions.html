Method of Selection

Candidates will be selected through a selection committee setup by the management or there may be a test to be conducted by the technical staff at this institute if required. More over preference will be given to the rural candidates ofsundargarh district. In no case admission shall be claimed as a matter of right purely on markbasis. The authority is empowered to refuse admission on specific ground and is not binding for admission. Canvassing in any form shall be treated as demerit for selection.

 

Age Limit

The candidates of 14 - 40 years of age are eligible to take admission as perthe norm of D.G.E. &T., Govt. of lndia.

 

Age Relaxation

(l) There is no objection to the state Govt. / Union Territory Administration making necessary relaxation of upper limit upto 45 yrs. in case of Ex-serviceman.

(ii) Relaxation of upper age limit upto 40 yrs. permissible in case ofwar widows.

(iii) Windows/Separated women would be allowed tojoin various training programme under C.T.S. upto theage of 35 years.

(iv) The upper age limit of physically handicapped candidates has been relaxed by 10 years and kept as 35years on the date from start admission session.
