About Us

Nuagaon Private Industrial Training Institute is a Training Center affiliated to N.C.V.T. & D.G.E.&T This Institute has been providing Craftsmen training in Fitter and Electrician Trade. Being a Non Profits seeking institution provides high skilled training in accordance to the syllabus provided byN.C.V.T. & D.G.E.&T,New Delhi, Ministry of labour, Government of India.

Chairman's Message

Nuagaon Private Industrial Training Institute was setup for promoting of Qualify Technical Education & Training in western Orissa.
