About Us

Nuagaon Private Industrial Training Institute is a Training Center affiliated to N.C.V.T. & D.G.E.&T This Institute has been providing Craftsmen training in Fitter and Electrician Trade . Being a Non Profits seeking institution provides high skilled training in accordance to the syllabus provided byN.C.V.T. & D.G.E.&T,New Delhi, Ministry of labour, Government of India.Â 


Nuagaon Private Industrial Training Institute is registered Under Trust which not only provided training in latest science & Technology but also creates skilled technician while imparting craftsmen training.The Institute inculcates moral values to the trainees to become an assets for various organisations as well as for the society.

 

OUR VISION

Our Thrust will be developing Institutional activities in Rural and Remote Area. Successful result oriented education Make technology Magic Roll of technology for nation.

 

LOCATION

This Institution is situated at Jamunadhipa (Kuarmunda) at a walkable distance from Vedvyas Chowk with pollution free atmosphere. The location map of this institution is show in contact pagefor better convenience forthe trainees.

 

AIMS & OBJECTIVES

The Aims of Craftsmen Training in Nuagaon Private Industrial Training Institute is to take part in our state as well as for National interest being encouraged by central Government and has established its reputation forhigh skilled training programe. The main objectives of NPITI is to provide skilled Technicians to work in various large and small scale industries & various organizations like RAILWAY, SAIL, NALCO, L & T WESCO, HAL and different Sponge Iron Factories etc. We believe in subjective and objective studies to improve the moral values of the trainees and to develop their personality.

 

Nuagaon Private Industrial Training Institute designed its infrasture with well equipped work shop ,class- Rooms, Audio Visuals, Library, Computer lab, canteen and hostel to provide better facilities to the trainees. This institute has also conducts various activities like Industrial Tour, Technical Seminars, Safety Demonstration, Essays, Debates, Quiz Competition and other curricular activities.

 

The main objective of NPITI is to ensure a steady flow of Skilled workers in different trades, to raise the quality and quantity of Industrial production by systematic training to the trainees and to reduce unemployment problem among the educated youth by equipping them suitable industrial employment as well as self employment.
